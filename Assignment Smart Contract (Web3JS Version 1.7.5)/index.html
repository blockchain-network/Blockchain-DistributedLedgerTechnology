What is DigiPortfolio

DigiPortfolio is an innovative platform for all to store their digital portfolio needs.

DigiPortfolio is one of the most important inventions in all of human history. For the first time ever, anyone can receive their digital credentials, anywhere on the planet, conveniently that is verified on the blockchain network.

Our Features

DigiPortfolio is the simplest way to retrieve your digital portfolio quickly and efficiently.

Safe & Secure

All information is stored in the blockchain network, ensuring that the information stored is final and immuatable.

Verifiable

All information stored in the blockchain network, has been verified by the parties governing the network.

Transparency

Information can be viewed by the recuiters and verify on the information that the job seeker had submitted.

Hassle Free

The job seekers can retrieve all their information from a centralized platform quickly and efficiently.

Final and Immuatable

The data in the blockchain is final and irreversible as all the participants in the same network holds the same identical copy of the ledger ensuring that there is only one source of truth in the network.

Provenance

Assets in the blockchain allows the participants in the network to know where the assets came from ensuring that the information is traceable on the blockchain.
